I. Introduction

A. Rationale

On-the-Job Training is a real-world experience for students to learn about the field they plan to enter. It helps in understanding workplace culture, expectations, and the skills needed to perform effectively in a professional environment. OJT provides opportunities to apply classroom knowledge, improve confidence, and build connections that can be valuable after graduation.

Internships help students learn, connect, and grow by developing skills outside the classroom and allowing exposure to real tasks and professional work standards.

B. Work / Training / Project Expectations

The practicum is expected to enhance technical competence, professional discipline, teamwork, communication skills, and adaptability in real-world information technology environments.

  • Apply IT knowledge in actual workplace tasks and workflows.
  • Strengthen documentation and communication skills.
  • Develop professionalism, accountability, and time management.
  • Improve troubleshooting and problem-solving abilities.

II. Organization Profile

A. Organization History and Background

It was Dr. Genevieve Ledesma-Laurel who founded in 1990 Southville International School and Colleges (SISC), which grew rapidly into a full international school. In 1997, Roger and Helen Bartholomew teamed up with the founder of SISC to start Southville International School Affiliated with Foreign Universities (SISFU), the first international university in the country. SISFU provides globally relevant education through partnerships with international institutions and rigorous academic standards.

Mission

SISFU, the premier transnational university in the Philippines, delivers globally-relevant quality undergraduate and graduate programs developed by accredited educational partners from the UK, Australia and the USA. Using rigorous international standards in instruction, assessment and research, supported by excellent facilities, highly-qualified faculty, and industry collaboration, SISFU prepares students to be globally competitive professionals, entrepreneurs, and leaders.

Vision

SISFU will be the leading transnational university that develops global leaders and professionals. It is distinguished by its excellent academic standards, outstanding instructional methodologies, relevant research, high levels of student achievement, a culture of innovation, and strong partnerships with ranked educational institutions.

B. Nature of Business

The institution operates as a higher education provider delivering undergraduate and graduate programs with international accreditation through partnerships with foreign universities.

III. Training / Project Profile

A. Training / Project Description

The practicum involved hands-on participation in IT support services, documentation, system maintenance, and administrative technical assistance. The experience provided exposure to daily IT operations and strengthened practical skills.

B. Nature of Training / Project

The training focused on practical application of information technology concepts, including troubleshooting, system support, user assistance, and data management aligned with workplace procedures.

IV. Observations

A. Competitive Advantages

The organization demonstrates efficiency through structured workflows, competent staff, and effective use of information systems. Clear coordination and established standards support smooth operations.

The practicum environment promotes professionalism, collaboration, and continuous improvement. The experience encouraged responsibility, communication, and adaptability while working on real tasks and deliverables.
